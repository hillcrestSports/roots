Stand Up Paddle Board Rentals

In Gresham OR | Near Sandy OR on the way to Mt Hood

Stand up paddle boarding has become quite popular recently in the world of watersports. Stand up paddle boarding (SUP) allows you explore the water without having to swim in it. It’s quite like surfing only you stand still and move yourself around using a paddle.

Paddle Board Rental

If you’re interested in paddle boarding at one of our local waterways, Hillcrest Sports provides stand up paddle board rentals. Our rentals are only $40 for the day. You’ll receive the paddle board (either inflatable or hardboard) a paddle, and your personal floatation device (FPD) all for 24 hours. If you need the equipment for more than 24 hours, we also provide longer rentals.

Where to Paddle Board

Here in the Portland OR and Vancouver WA area, there are several popular places to go paddle boarding. Take in the breathtaking views of Mt. Hood at Trillium Lake located in the Mt. Hood National Forest or stay closer to home and head out to Vancouver Lake -- which is a great spot for beginners. Most places require a day use fee, but to make it convenient, we sell recreational passes in-store. So when you stop by to pick up your rental, you can pick up your pass at the same time!

Transporting Your Paddle Board

One thing to keep in mind with your paddle board rental is how you’ll be transporting it. You can invest in a rack to fit in the back of your truck or car or invest in a small trailer that’s capable of transporting several paddle boards at one time. You can also tie it down by using straps and the crossbars installed on the roof of your vehicle.

Reserve Your Paddle Board

Once you’re ready to give stand up paddle boarding a try, contact Hillcrest Sports for your paddle board rental. We’ll teach you the basics and then have you on your way. We’re conveniently located at the corner of Powell and Burnside at the beginning of Hwy 26. We look forward to seeing you!
