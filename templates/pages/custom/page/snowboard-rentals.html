Snowboard Rentals

In Gresham OR | Near Sandy OR on the way to Mt Hood

Hillcrest Ski & Sports has everything you need for your next snowboarding adventure in the Portland area! Our snowboard packages include men’s snowboards, women’s snowboards, and kid’s snowboards complete with boots and bindings. Have your own snowboard and just need boots? We also rent out single equipment including men’s snowboard boots, women’s snowboard boots, and kid’s snowboard boots. If you’re new to the sport, we’ll help make sure you’re properly fitted to your boots and snowboard. We also offer demonstration snowboard packages. We rent out our equipment on a first-come-first-serve basis and once you receive your snowboard equipment, you’ll have it for a full 24 hours, however, longer rentals are available. Hillcrest Ski & Sports is Portland’s one-stop-shop for all your winter sports needs. We also provide recreational passes! We are conveniently located at the corner of Powell and Burnside at the beginning of Hwy 26. So contact us today at 503-665-4455 for your snowboard rentals!

Snowboard Package Rentals

Different boards are designed for different skill levels. And we at Hillcrest cater to them all. We provide snowboards for beginners, intermediate, advanced, and junior boarders. Keep in mind if you’re only doing a snowboard rental, you’ll want to bring your boots in at the time of pick-up so they can be properly fitted. If you’re getting the snowboard rental package, the fitting is already included.

Snow Gear for Rent

Be out on the slopes for as long as you want with our snowboard boot rental. We provide high-quality waterproof boots in several different brands and models to cater to everyone’s shape, size, and skill level. We want you to be comfortable during your snowboarding adventure, which is why we not only fit the boots to you, but to your snowboard as well.
