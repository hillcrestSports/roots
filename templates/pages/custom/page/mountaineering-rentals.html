Mountaineering Rentals

In Gresham OR | Near Sandy OR on the way to Mt Hood

No matter how much experience you have, you will want to bring mountaineering gear to your trip. Boots: Not only do you want boots that fit comfortably, but you also want a pair with good traction that will keep you from slipping on the ice. You’ll need the boot to be tougher, more supportive and have a stiffer sole to accommodate crampons. We have a brand new fleet of both men’s and women’s La Sportiva Trango Tower Boots. The Trango Tower is the perfect 3-season alpine boot for tackling any of our local Cascade giants such as Mt Hood or Mt Adams.



Mountaineering Package Rentals

  • Crampons: These work with your boots and have sharp metal spikes on the bottom that help you sink your feet into the ice. Crampons are built in a variety of materials dependent upon the activity. Hillcrest rents semi-rigid, steel crampons in both universal straps and semi automatic straps that will fit a wide range of sole types. Hillcrest has a brand new fleet of the CAMP Stalker Univeral and CAMP Stalker Semi-Auto.
  • Ice ax: Use an ice ax to break through the ice and keep your balance as well as to self arrest during a sudden fall. Ice axes are sized based on intended activity and height of user. Hillcrest currently stocks the CAMP Neve in a wide variety of sizes.
  • Helmet: Protect yourself against loose, falling rocks and ice with a helmet. All Mountaineering helmets are certified for overhead protection unlike most ski and snowboard helmets which are certified for side-protection. We offer the CAMP Titan helmets. Though you can buy mountaineering equipment, you can save some money when you rent gear instead. Hillcrest has all the mountaineering equipment you need that you can rent for 48 hours. To reserve the equipment, stop by our store!
