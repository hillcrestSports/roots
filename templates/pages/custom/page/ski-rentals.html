Ski Rentals

In Gresham OR | Near Sandy OR on the way to Mt Hood

Enjoy the slopes with Hillcrest Ski & Sports’ ski rentals! We offer a variety of ski equipment rentals including men’s skis and boots, women’s skis and boots, and kid’s skis and boots. Every ski package also comes with bindings and poles. New to skiing? We have experienced trainers to properly fit you for your equipment-- depending on your skill level, and they’ll also provide a demonstration for you. All rentals are on a first-come, first-serve basis. Once you rent your ski equipment, you’ll have it for 24 hours, however, longer rentals are available. Hillcrest Ski & Sports is your one-stop-shop for all your skiing needs as we also sell recreational passes. So contact us today at 503-665-4455 for your ski rentals. We are located at the corner of Powell and Burnside at the beginning of Hwy 26.

Ski Package Rental

To have an enjoyable skiing experience, it’s important to have a pair of skis that are the perfect length for you. To do this, we at Hillcrest Sports will measure your height and weight, learn your skill level as well as learn what terrain you’ll be on. The type of skis we’ll give you will be based on the type of skiing you plan to do.
  • All-Mountain skis are great for a mix of terrain including groomed and powder. They are suited for all skill levels and age groups.
  • All-Mountain wide skies work well in groomed areas and powder areas. They are slightly wider in the waist than the all-mountain ski.
  • Powder skis are best if you plan on skiing in deep powder snow.


    • Rental Ski Boots

Your ski boots are perhaps the most important part of your rental package. If they’re too stiff, too small or too large, you will not have a great experience. This is why we at Hillcrest Sports always have you try on the boot first and if it’s not comfortable, we’ll try other sizes and models until we find you a perfect match. Everyone has different feet sizes and shapes as well as skill levels when it comes to skiing and all should be taken into consideration when selecting a boot.

Rental Ski Poles

Your ski poles help you balance and time your turns. Whether you’ve forgotten, misplaced, or broke your own ski poles, we at Hillcrest provide rental ski poles for the low price of $7. We have adult and children’s ski poles for rent.
