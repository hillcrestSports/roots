Kayak Rentals

In Gresham OR | Near Sandy OR on the way to Mt Hood

Kayaking is a watersport that allows you to enjoy nature from a different perspective. It can be a calm and relaxing experience set on a mellow lake or a fun and exciting adventure on the rapids of your local river. You decide. The possibilities for kayaking are endless. If you’re thinking about kayaking, Hillcrest Sports offers daily and seasonal kayak rentals. We carry a full range of kayaks including single kayaks and tandem kayaks-- so you can explore alone or with a friend! Our kayak rentals start at the low price of $ 40 and come with everything you need to have a safe kayaking experience including your paddles and your personal flotation device (PFD). We also offer recreational passes in-store.

Where to Kayak

There are several popular kayaking spots in the Portland OR and Vancouver WA area. Whether you want to stay close to home and kayak on Sauvie Island or Vancouver Lake or venture out toward the mountains and try Trillium Lake and Frog Lake, we will help you find an ideal kayaking spot catered to your skill level. Remember recreation passes can be purchased at our store as well, so pick up your pass and head to one of the many popular kayaking destinations our beautiful area has to offer.

Transport Kayaks

If you’re thinking about renting a kayak, you’ll want to consider how you’re going to transport it. There are a variety of ways to safely transport a kayak. Kayaks can be tied down to your vehicle with cam straps and water-resistant rope. You can also invest in a roof rack or smaller trailer to making transporting your kayak effortless. To reserve your kayak rental call us at 503-665-4455 or stop by Hillcrest Sports. We’re conveniently located at the corner of Powell and Burnside at the beginning of Hwy 26.
